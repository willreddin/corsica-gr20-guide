A guide to the Corsican GR20

Stage by stage

Introduction

This site gives a stage by stage guide to Corsica's GR20, a 180km long distance path that traverses the mountain ranges down the length of the island.

The route has a reputation of being the toughest of the "Grande Randonn√©e" footpaths in Europe. Some stages particularly in the Northern sections will require some scrambling and crossing very rocky terrain. But with a bit of preparation it truly is one of the best treks in the world.

The route can be split into 15 stages with a refuge or camping site at the end of each stage. Most people take 15 days to complete the route, walking a stage per day.
